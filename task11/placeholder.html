CSS Outline Property

The outline property draws a line around elements, outside the border. It is not part of the box model and does not take up space. It's often used for accessibility (e.g., focus indicators).

outline-style and outline-width

Dashed Outline (3px)
Solid Outline (4px)
Dotted Outline (2px)

outline-color and Shorthand

Blue Solid Outline
Red Double Outline
Yellow Groove Outline
